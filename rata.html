How to Take a Course

Taking a course with RATA is straightforward and accessible. Follow these steps:

Navigate to the course you are interested in from the list above.
Click on "View Course" and you will move to login page.
If you don't have account on rata academy click on "Sign Up" to create it.
Complete the modules at your own pace. Each module includes video(not yet) lectures(not yet), reading materials, and quizzes (not yet).

Certification Process

Upon successful completion of a course, you will be eligible for a certification. Here's how you can obtain it:

  • Complete all course modules and pass the final assessment.
  • Submit any required assignments.
  • Certificate will be issued upon successful completion if you passed with above 75%.

Your certificate will be available for download in PDF format under certificates under sidebar.

Display your certificate on your LinkedIn profile or share it with your employer.
